What is included in a Doggie Walk



Once a Week

A good 30 minutes walk if the heath of the dog is able. Prefer 15 minutes of some type of favorite play time like fetch, tug of war, getting some pets etc. Then a nice 15 minute cool down.


Twice a Week

All the same things as the once a week. Additionally, the belly rubs and play times last 10 to 15 minutes longer depending on how interested your four legged friend is.


Three Times a Week

Everything listed above is included in this section from Once a week and twice a week.


Five Time a Week

We will spend a full two hours. Your four legged friend will receive a 30 to 45 minute walk. A good 30 minuties playing outside with our staff throwing a ball or playing with their favorite toy. Wrapping up up with the rest of the time getting belly rubs and lots of love and attention.
