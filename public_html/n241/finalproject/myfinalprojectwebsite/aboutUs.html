About Us


Roberto Kuhn


Roberto has over 25 years in the show rings and training areas. He has won nine Championship titles and ten Obedience title. The highest award in obedience was a Optch with standard poodle. (Notch is scent work.) Roberto offers classes and private lessons. His number one goal is to help you and your new four legged best friend bond for a life time of joy. If interested in his classes sign up fast, his lessons fill up quickly.


Mary Henderson


Mary has over 10 years of experience in the obedience rings. She is amazing with terriers and dogs with a strong personality. She has Jack Russells and is well known in the Jack Russell Terrier Club of America (JRTCA). Mary only does group classes. She has found that with strong personality puppies they need the group socialization more than private one-on-one attention.
