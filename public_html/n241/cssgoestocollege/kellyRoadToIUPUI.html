My Journey to IUPUI



The story of me going back to higher education. I was once told - One is never too old to learn, which brings me to today.

The Journey to IUPUI:

  • Started back in 1987.
  • Worked as a Corporate Trainer for 30 years.
  • Retired as a VP in Training and Development.
  • Took care of a sick family member.
  • Worked on our home in Houston after family member had to move into assisted living.
  • Was told our house was not my personal projects.
  • Next day registed at LoneStar College.
  • Searched for a good University Program in Computer Science.
  • Moved all our lives to Indianapolis, IN.

Background Information


I spent some time in Ethiopia before starting Kindergarten with my parents. I Graduated High School in 1987 where I moved to San Antonio, TX to attend Trinity University. I wanted to major in Computer Science but my family made it clear there was no money to be made in that field and forced me to get a degree in Business Administration. They made it clear, We are paying for your education you will get a degree in the field we tell you. So, after Trinity University I stayed in San Antonio for 10 years cutting my teeth at my first job. In 2000, I moved to Austin, TX with the .com boom. Stayed in Austin, TX where I met my future partner. Stayed in Austin for 10 years and moved from being a Trainer, Training Manager, Director of Training, and VP of Training and Development. After 10 years in Austin, I moved to Houston, TX with my partner. We spent 10 years in Houston, TX where the second half of going back to school.
