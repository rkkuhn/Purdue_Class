Introduction to Jack Russell Dogs



You will never know the joy of owning a dog that is loyal, lovable, and high energy. Jack Russells are the best dog and worst dog you will ever own. I have this to be so true about the breed.

Traits of an Jack Russell

  1. Friendless - mild mannor
  2. Exercise Needs - high
  3. Health Issues - middle of the road
  4. Barking Tendencies - very high
  5. Grooming Needs - low
  6. Good with Cat - NO
  7. Playfulness - very high
  8. Weather Preference - adapts to either cold or hot
  9. Good for First Time Pet Owner - NOT AT ALL

Here is some interesting information about the history of the breed


The Jack Russell Terrier was developed in England some 200 years ago to hut foxes. This dog is a lively, independent, and clever little creature. They're charming and affectionate, BUT they are a handful to train and manage. This breed is for experienced dog parents only!
